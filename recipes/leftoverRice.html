Leftover Rice Mixed Fried Rice


Ingredients:

  • Leftover Rice
  • Chopped Onions
  • Eggs
  • Sausage
  • Peas, carrots, broccoli or vegtables of your choice
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Tumeric
  • Cumin powder
  • Masala Powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter or choice of cooking oil
  • Parsley or scallions

Steps:

  1. Heat up some cooking oil in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic and cook in medium heat until brown
  3. Add your vegetables and sautee them until cooked.
  4. Add salt, pepper, tumeric, cumin powder and masala powder andd mix well.
  5. Move the cooked vegetables towards the edges of the pan and add some oil in the middle.
  6. Crack two eggs in a bowl and whisk them well with salt and pepper.
  7. Pour the eggs into middle of the pan and scramble them.
  8. Once they are almost done, mix it well with the vegtables and cook for few minutes.
  9. Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  10. Break up the rice and add to the pan.
  11. Mix well and cook on medium heat until rice starts to brown.
  12. Repeat step number 3 according to taste.
  13. Serve in a bowl and top off with scallions or parsley and enjoy!
