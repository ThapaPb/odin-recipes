Nepali Chicken Recipe


Description

Chicken is one of the staple dishes of Nepali household. It is normally made in a curry style and is a household favorite. I used to desperately wait for Saturdays because my mom would make her famous Chicken every Saturday. It is served with freshly cooked rice, two or three different types of stir-fry vegetables, lentil soup and pickled relish.


Ingredients"

  • Chicken thigh pieces
  • Cooking oil
  • Onions finely chopped
  • Garlic finely chopped
  • Tomatoes
  • Tumeric
  • Cumin powder
  • Ginger powder
  • Chicken masala
  • Prasley
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat up some cooking oil in a pan and add chopped onions
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook on high heat.
  3. Stir the chicken until water evporates and you can see the oil.
  4. Add teh chopped garlic and cook until brown.
  5. Lower the heat to medium and add salt.
  6. Add tumeric, cumin powder, ginger powder, pepper and chicken masala.
  7. Stir until all the spices are mixed evenly.
  8. Put the tomatoes in a blender and make a paste.
  9. Pour the tomatoes into the pan with the chicken and mix well
  10. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes.
  11. Add freshly chopped parsley, cover the pan and leave it on low heat for another 10 minutes.
  12. Serve the chicken with some rice and enjoy!
