Delicious Oats Breakfast


Description

Oats make for the perfect quick, easy and healthy breakfast. This recipie will make your Oats taste delicious and enjoyable!


Ingredients:

  • Old-fashioned Oats
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Fresh or frozed fruits
  • Nuts and seeds mixed

Steps:

  1. Melt some butter in a non-stick pan
  2. Add Cinnamon powder to the butter and mix well.
  3. Add half a cup of oats into the pan and mix well on high heat for 3 minutes.
  4. Add a cup of water and a pinch of salt and mix well on high heat for 3 minutes.
  5. Lower heat to low and cook covered for 5 minutes.
  6. Let the oats cool off for about 5 minutes.
  7. Serve in a bowl and add fruits of your choice.
  8. Finally add some mixed nuts and seeds and enjoy!
